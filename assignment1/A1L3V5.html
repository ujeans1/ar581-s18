THE
NUMBER
WERE IT
TO EXIST
other than as
a scattered hallucination
of dying
WERE IT TO
BEGIN AND END
rising only
to be denied
and closed off
when revealed

at last

by some thinly
spread profusion

WERE IT TO BE COUNTED

evidence of
the sum
however small

WERE IT TO ILLUMINATE
